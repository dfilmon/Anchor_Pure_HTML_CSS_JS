FAQ

What Type of Help Can I Expect from the Career Mentors?

Our world class Mentors are hand selceted by our talent acquisition team. They are prepared to assist you with understanding the results that you receive on your Career Test. They help you determine which career best fits your personality, goals, and aspirations based on the careers given in your results. They assist with navigating what credientials you may need, and where to obtain those, as well as helping you with buliding your personal brand, and resueme. They walk with you step by step preparing you for employment, the hiring process, and continue to follow up with you throughout your first year of employment.

What if I do not agree with the career test results?
  • The Career test results are just a guide to help assist you with matching a career to your personailty type.
  • The Career test does not determine which Career you choose to pursue, you are the finial decision maker in what career you choose to pursue.
  • You are able to discuss with our mentors in detail your test results and why you may agree or disagree with the results.
How does AnchorCareer support me in efforts to find a career?

At AnchorCareer's our goal is to assist you with determining your dream career. We have created an interactive webpage that will assit you from start to finish with choosing a career that your love so much that you want to show up everyday to work even if you were not receiving compensation for your time. We have made avaliable a career test, in depth exploration of various career, and access to world class career mentors to assist you with transitioning into your dream career.
